ADMISSION FOR STUDENTS

In Higher Education

The UGC issued COVID-19 specific guidelines for Indian Higher Education Institutions (HEIs) has resulted into temporarily closing of ~1000 Universities and ~ 40,000 colleges while students going home. This year, close to 1.44 crore students are appearing for their school leaving exams and close to 60 lakhs of these students will be looking to enroll in a higher education institution. India Education Week platform will help the students to find the college/university of their own choice and get their admission done.

EDUCATIN LOAN

For Students

Forget the hassle of running pillar to post to get your education loan approval. India Education Week presents to you the opportunity to connect with different banks on the spot at college campuses. All you need to do is select your preferred course at your preferred institute or college and get instant information on different types of loan structures and EMI options.

Remote Examination

At the same, it brought unprecedented opportunity which the world could only foresee 4-5 years down the line. As the academics stated setting up their telescope for delivering remote education, examination and evaluation of the candidate became the need of the hour and a new wave in the form of Remote Proctoring emerged to provide hasslefree and controlled examination setting remotly. IEW shall be presenting the right technology partners in the field of Remote Proctoring for robust examination setting in your college/univesity

EDTECH COMPANIES

Transforming Education

A report by RedSeer and Omidyar Network India has mapped the growth in usage from 2019 to 2020. It revealed that edtech users - both paid and free unique users - in K12 and post K-12 segment have seen an increase, with the user base doubling from 45 million to 90 million. There also has been a 50 percent increase in time spent, which has gone up from 60 minutes to 90 minutes. The report also noted a 40 percent increase in willingness to pay and a massive 83 percent jump in the paid user base. According to the report, online education offerings across Classes 1 to 12 are projected to increase 6.3 times by 2022, to create a $ 1.7 billion market. The post-K12 market is set to grow 3.7 times to touch $1.8 billion. This will create meaningful opportunities for incumbent players and space for multiple new startups.

ERP FOR

College Management

As the college and universities goes online, web based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) application is the one kind of web application which integrates all the modules and functionalities of college system on a single system that can be handled by the administrative head and access by the students and faculties. However old in legacy, but a larger colleges and universitites have been found oblivious about the features and benefits which can be leveraged by academic settings. India Education Summit will be showcasing the world class ERP pioneers in the education industry who can help you streamline your academic operations and student engagement efficiently

ENTERPRISE SOLUTIONS

In Academic Setting

Enterprise solutions could help restore some much-needed external and internal work-life balance by helping admins, employees, professors and students become much more proactive, better able to plan out their work day, and work more productively. The hope is that as enterprise solution in academic settings become smarter, they’ll do many quotidian tasks, like integrations across data, improve cross-departmental communication, cooperation, and efficiency. No more late nights slogging over data reports, in other words.

The point of enterprise technology in education setting is to make working better—more efficient, less stressful, safer, and maybe even fun—and coming innovations in technology will be part of the future of work. “Technology should not be used as a way to engineer humans out. It should be a way to lift them up and to use all that is great about humanity to do business and do business well.
