What are the dates of the conference?

The event will be hosted as a virtual conference and an exhibition, with live admission process, keynotes, live panel discussion, live exhibitions all at the comfort of your office or home.

ADMISSION
What are the courses available in the exhibition?
All types of higher education courses are available for your admission like B.E., B.Tech., B.B.A, B.C.A, M.B.A., M.C.A. etc.
What type of college/universities will be present in the exhibition?
All prominemt colleges and universities of India from different states and cities will be present in the exhibition like Pune and Mumbai from Maharashtra, Bangalore from Karnataka, Hyderabad from Telangana etc.
Can I get spot admission in my preferred course in the admission?
Yes, you can get the admission on the spot in any course in any college as per your need.
What are the criteria for taking admission in any course?
You need to fulfill the eligibility criteria for the respective course of any given college/university. The eligibility criteria will be published by all the colleges and universities on the day of the exhibition.
What are the loan options available for admission in any college/university?
You can enquire about various educational loan options provided by different banks in the exhibition. You can directly talk to college representative or bank representatives live and get all information about your loan requirements.
